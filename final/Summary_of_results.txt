Result Summary – Final Prototype
The final HydrateMate prototype was successfully developed and made accessible through a public Figma link for stakeholder review. The prototype demonstrates the core functionality of the application and simulates a mobile user experience through an interactive interface.
Users are able to register and log in using their email or phone number before accessing the main homepage. The homepage displays the user’s daily water goal and hydration progress, allowing users to log their water intake quickly based on different cup sizes. The prototype also includes several supporting features designed to encourage consistent hydration habits, such as streak tracking, water intake history, and water facts pop-ups that provide educational information about hydration.
In addition, the application incorporates gamification elements through a badge reward system where users can earn and view badges for their achievements. A social motivation feature is also included, allowing users to invite friends and share achievements to encourage consistent water tracking. Other usability features such as notification reminders and dark mode are available through the settings page to enhance user experience.
During the final testing stage, several usability improvements were implemented. Back buttons were added to pages such as View History, Streaks, Badge Collection, and Settings to improve navigation efficiency and reduce reliance on the main navigation menu.
Overall, the final prototype demonstrates a functional and user-friendly mobile application concept that enables users to monitor their daily water intake, stay motivated through gamified features, and maintain healthier hydration habits.
