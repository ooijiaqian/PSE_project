	1	INTRODUCTION OF HYDRATEMATE
HydrateMate is a gamified water intake tracking application designed . The app transforms the routine task of drinking water into a fun and rewarding experience through badge collection, streak tracking, and visual progress indicators.
By making hydration engaging and interactive, HydrateMate aims to help students build consistent drinking habits, improve concentration and energy levels, and enhance overall well-being throughout their academic journey.

	2	TARGET GROUP & NEEDS
Target Groups and Their Needs:
Office Workers
	•	Sit for long hours at desks
	•	Frequently forget to drink water
	•	Experience headaches and eye strain from prolonged screen exposure
Busy Parents
	•	Focus heavily on childcare responsibilities
	•	Often neglect their own hydration
	•	Require reminders to maintain healthy habits
Fitness Enthusiasts
	•	Require increased hydration after exercise
	•	Need accurate tracking of daily water intake
Elderly
	•	Often forget to drink water
	•	Require gentle reminders
	•	Higher risk of dehydration
Healthcare Workers
	•	Work long and demanding shifts
	•	Limited time for self-care
	•	Need structured hydration reminders
Remote Workers / Work From Home (WFH)
	•	Lack structured daily routine
	•	Easily overlook basic needs while working

	3	PRODUCT GOALS
Make drinking water fun
	•	Implement badge collection and streak systems
Make logging easy
	•	Enable one tap = one cup logging
Keep users motivated
	•	Provide visual progress bars and achievement feedback
Remind users
	•	Send push notifications for hydration reminders
Show users progress
	•	Provide weekly reports and analysis charts

	4	BUSINESS GOALS
Encourage Daily Logging
	•	Target: 75% – 80% of users log water daily
Maintain User Streaks
	•	Target: Minimum 7 consecutive days
Achieve Positive User Feedback
	•	Target: 4–5 star rating
Complete Functional Prototype
	•	Target: Minimum 10 features by Week 5

	5	KEY FEATURES
One Tap Water Logging
	•	Log water intake quickly using a single tap
Set Daily Water Goal
	•	Users can define their daily cup target
Progress Display
	•	Show percentage of daily goal achieved
Earn Badges
	•	Unlock rewards after reaching milestones
Receive Notifications
	•	Reminder alerts to drink water
View History
	•	Access previous daily records and analysis charts
Log Different Cup Sizes
	•	Small, medium, large, or custom cup options
Customize Goal by Activity Level
	•	Adjust water intake goals based on activity level
View Badge Collection
	•	Display earned and locked badges for motivation
Compete with Friends
	•	Leaderboard system to challenge friends
Water Facts Popup
	•	Display educational hydration facts while logging
Streaks Display
	•	Track consecutive days of achieving goal
Dark Mode
	•	User-friendly interface for night usage
